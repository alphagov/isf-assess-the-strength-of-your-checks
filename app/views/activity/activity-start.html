Activity checks

Check the identity has existed over time

You should check if the person has interacted with other organisations over time.

This will help you to be sure that the identity you're checking is real. 'Synthetic' (or made up) identities are unlikely to have records in the real world.

Some examples of interactions that can prove someone's identity include:

  • credit card transactions
  • student loan repayments
  • mortgage payments
  • gas or electricity account payments

You can also check if any records exist that show if the person has been known by other organisations over time, such as their:

  • health records
  • employment records
  • school records

The highest score you can get for this check is 4.
