Activity checks

Have you found interactions that you'd expect that person to have?

Some people's interactions will be different from others. The interactions you expect someone to have will depend on things like:

  • the type of interaction they're doing
  • the type of person they are (like their age or financial situation)

For example, you can expect people who have been working for 20 years to have more frequent financial transactions than people who have recently left school.

If the interactions are not what you'd expect, you can still use them to check if someone exists. You might need to look for interactions over a longer time period.
