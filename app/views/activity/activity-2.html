Activity checks

Have you found interactions that you’d expect that person to have?

Some people’s interactions might appear differently to others. The interactions you expect someone to have will usually depend on:

  • the type of interaction
  • who they are (their interactions can depend on things like their age or financial situation)

For example, you can expect someone who's been working for 20 years to have frequent financial transactions, but someone who's recently left school might not.

If you can't find interactions that happen in the way you'd expect for that person, you might be less sure they were actually involved in them.
