How are ‘false matches’ and ‘false non-matches’ handled in your system?

A ‘false match rate’ happens when a person's biometric information appears to be the same as biometric information that belongs to someone else.

A ‘false non-match’ happens when someone’s biometric information does not match biometric information that your system has previously collected.
