Verification checks

How does the system check the person is real?

Your system can do a ‘liveness’ check to confirm that the person is real. A ‘basic’ liveness check could be something like using a heart rate sensor to check the person is alive when it takes their fingerprint.

Your service will do an ‘enhanced’ liveness check if it uses multiple process or measures to confirm the person is real. This could be something like asking the person to take a short video of themselves, during which they repeat a random sequence of words back to you.
