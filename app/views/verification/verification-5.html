Are the KBV questions 'static' or 'dynamic'?

A KBV question is 'static' if the information the person needs to answer it does not change over time. This could be something like their date of birth.

A KBV question is 'dynamic' if the answer does change over time, for example the amount on the person's monthly credit card statement. These sort of questions are harder to be answered by impostors using information from things like data breaches.
