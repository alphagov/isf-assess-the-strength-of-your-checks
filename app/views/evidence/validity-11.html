What other checks does the person or system do?

They should check all of the following features on the document:

  • watermarks
  • ultraviolet security features
  • security fibres
  • consistency across the whole document

To check these, they must use:

  • an ultraviolet desktop light
  • a magnification tool (such as a magnifier)
  • other inspection equipment to identify forged or counterfeit documents
  • an oblique light
