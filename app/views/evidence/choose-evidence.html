What type of evidence do you collect?

Some pieces of evidence are stronger than others, which means they will be harder to fake or copy. How strong the evidence is depends on:

  • what security features (like a hologram or an electronic chip) are on it
  • what information is on it
  • how it was designed
  • how it was issued

You'll get a higher score if your service collects strong pieces of evidence. The highest score you can get for this check is 4.
