Some pieces of evidence are 'stronger' than others, which means they will be harder to:

  • forge
  • counterfeit

How strong the evidence is depends on:

  • what security features are on it (for example a hologram or an electronic chip)
  • what information it has
  • how the person proved their identity to get the evidence

The stronger the evidence is, the higher its score will be. The highest score you can get for this check is 4.

You must check and score one piece of evidence at a time.

If you collect different combinations of evidence, you should check and score one combination at a time.
