Assess the strength of your identity checks

Use this service to find out:

  • how well your current process checks users’ identities
  • if your current process is at risk of identity fraud

This service is based on the UK government's Identity Standards, which help organisations decide the best way to check identity.

What you’ll need to do

You’ll need to answer questions about how you check users’ identities.

To do this, you need to know what documents your service collects and how it makes sure if they’re genuine and valid.

You’ll also need to know if your service checks:

  • the user’s identity has existed over time
  • the user’s identity hasn’t been stolen or used fraudulently
  • the identity shown on any documents your service collects belongs to the same person that claims to have it

What you’ll get

  1. You’ll get a score for each part of the identity checking process your service does. How high the score is will depend on how thoroughly you do the check. The highest score you can get is 4.
  2. Your scores will make up an ‘identity profile’. This tells you how confident you can be that the person you're checking is who they say they are.
  3. You’ll see what other identity profiles you can meet. If you do not meet any of them, you’ll find out which checks you need to improve.
