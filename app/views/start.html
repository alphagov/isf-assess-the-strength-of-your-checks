Assess the strength of your identity checks

Use this service to find out:

  • how well your current process checks users’ identities
  • if your current process is at risk of identity fraud

This service is based on the UK government's Identity Standards, which help organisations decide the best way to check identity.

What you’ll get

  1. You’ll get a score for each part of the identity checking process you do.
  2. Your scores will make up an ‘identity profile’.
  3. You’ll see other identity profiles and find out what checks you need to improve to meet them.
